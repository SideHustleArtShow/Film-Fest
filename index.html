Submissions must abide by the following:

  • An original film of your own creation (get creative, go wild!)
  • All genres and styles welcome (live action or animated)
  • Rated Gâ€“R (no NC17, extreme violence, pornographic scenes, or hate speech)
  • Captured with any recording device (digital or analog)
  • Color or B&W
  • Shot and produced between September 2024 and June 2025
  • Not be publicly premiered prior to SHFF
  • Applicant is responsible for getting all proper licensing permission(s)
  • Applicant resides in the US and is 18+ years old

SHFF reserves the right to deny any film submission for any reason.
